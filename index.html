Why wait for the customer to come to the show when you can take the show to the customer?

Our exhibition vehicle gives you the perfect opportunity to come & present your technology to all those important people who do not manage to get to the traditional trade show anymore. Our marketing activities are curtailed - This is an innovative way to market. Lastly, the supplier engagement is reduced - re-engage with both new and existing customers.
